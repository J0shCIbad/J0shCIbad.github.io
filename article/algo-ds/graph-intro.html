Graph Algorithms 

Introduction

Introduction:

Graph and network algorithms constitute an essential category of algorithms upon which a great many modern technologies depend on. With graphs and networks being the primary data structure used to represent relationships and connections between entities, they are used to model or computationally represent concrete objects like circuits, routes, computer networks, artificial neural networks, and even tasks.

The algorithms designed to operate on these data structures define how accurate and efficient machines will be able to route vehicles, handle logistics, plan or schedule tasks, establish optimal assignment of workers to positions. Even the internet itself depends on graph algorithms, be it for TCP/IP routing protocols which facilitates network communication, or for assigning clients to internet service provider servers to accomodate the vast number of users needing to establish connection to the internet.
